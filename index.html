What is the BackUppProject about?

We live in a world full of complexity. Mankind and its potential evolve so fast that we fear to get suspended. We strive for more and more knowledge - every day. Technology becomes a dominating part of our lives and jobs. Therefore, for most of us, it is essential to have at least a general understanding of new technologies like AI, IT or CS. We have to keep updated, with the latest news and developments in a special field of technology.

For that reason we created an APP that covers many different fields of technology. It provides you with with the latest Tech-Information. Additionally, everyone who has to offer knowledge - professionals, students or youtubers - are welcome to share their knowledge on the platform.

Join us. Stand up for the freedom of information. Share your knowledge. Learn from the community.
